What Is Random Card Poker?

A proprietary poker-style casino game prepared for operator evaluation.

Players place an initial BET, receive four private cards and decide whether to PLAY or FOLD. The Botón D™ then activates a blind selection of the community card that completes every active hand.

The result is a familiar poker-style experience with a unique reveal mechanic, clear table flow and potential adaptation for land-based casino tables, live dealer studios and digital RNG environments.
